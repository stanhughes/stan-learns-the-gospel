Welcome Message , by President Russell M. Nelson

This is a very short talk. The following points caught my attention.

We gather as a great global family desiring to worship our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ.

The desire is the why of what we are doing and is very important. If the desire is wrong then the activity cannot be blessed by God. It is very difficult to determine someone desire; However, it is clear to God, Who grants unto us the righteous desires of our hearts, see Alma 41:3, 5-6
